Aquiring content of registers with the USS-protocol

VS2010 project. This program displays the content of registers of the MicroMaster inverter on the PC screen.
Must connect the MicroMaster device to the rs232-rs485 pcb according to the schematic: schematic-rs232-rs485.pdf
The DSUB connector goes to the serial port COM1 of PC.
